About

Why my perspective
is different

I'm Luiz — Brazilian, based between Dublin, Ireland and São Paulo, Brazil. Over 17 years I've worked inside some of the world's leading digital platforms, global media agencies across Europe, and as an independent consultant serving clients in both markets.

Most advisors come from one world — agency, platform, or consultant. I've done all three, in two very different markets. That combination means I understand how digital advertising actually works from the inside, what agencies say versus what they deliver, and what business owners and founders genuinely need to hear — not what sounds good in a deck.

I speak English and Portuguese fluently, which means I work equally well with Brazilian companies expanding internationally, European businesses entering LATAM, and individuals navigating either market.

Outside of work: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, surfing, and a genuine obsession with behavioural economics. Both BJJ and surfing teach the same lesson — systems and consistency beat raw talent every time.
