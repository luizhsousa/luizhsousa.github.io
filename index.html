About

Built on
real experience

I'm Luiz — Brazilian, currently between Dublin and Sao Paulo. Over 17 years I've worked inside some of the world's leading digital platforms, global media agencies, and as an independent consultant across European and Latin American markets.

That range — platform, agency, independent — gives me a perspective most advisors don't have. I've seen what works from every angle. I know what the platforms are optimising for, what agencies present versus what they deliver, and what founders actually need to hear.

I believe good strategy is simple to explain. If you can't say it clearly, the thinking isn't there yet. That's the standard I hold my own work to — and the one I bring to every client conversation.

Outside of work: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, surfing, and a genuine obsession with behavioural economics. Both BJJ and surfing taught me that systems beat raw talent every time — and that the moment you think you've figured it out, the water has other plans.
